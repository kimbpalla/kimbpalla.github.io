Featured Movies

Tangled 2010

Tangled tells the story of Rapunzel, a lost young princess with magical long blonde hair who yearns to leave her secluded tower. She accepts the aid of an intruder, the outlaw Flynn Rider, to take her out into the world which she has never seen. Together, they embark on an adventure that challenges their beliefs and brings them closer to discovering their true selves.

Tangled Ever After 2012

Tangled Ever After is a 2012 Disney short film that takes place about three years after the events of the original Tangled. The short film is about the royal wedding of Rapunzel and Eugene Fitzherbert, and the search for the wedding rings after they are lost by Pascal the chameleon and Maximus the horse.

Tangled Before Ever After 2017

Tangled: Before Ever After (2017) is an animated television movie that serves as a prequel to the movie Tangled. It follows the adventures of Rapunzel as she adjusts to life with her newly restored long hair and navigates her royal duties as princess.
